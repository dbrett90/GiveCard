GiveCard Campus Ambassador Application

Are you...

- A college student eager to make an impact? -
- Passionate about breaking the cycle of homelessness? -
- Connected across your campus and on social media? -
- Seeking hands-on business experience and networking opportunities? -

GiveCard is looking for driven and compassionate college students for our first campus ambassador program. You’ll represent the GiveCard brand and further our mission of financial empowerment for homeless communities. Specifically, you’ll work on a small team at your school and build a marketing plan to drive engagement and funding for our cashless solutions. You’ll gain tangible skills to help jumpstart your career, exclusive access to the founding team, and even some fun rewards along the way (including exclusive hiring events with Fortune 500 companies and some generous promotions from our corporate sponsors). The program runs 3/1-5/31.

Mission

We’re a nonprofit organization giving specialized debit cards, digital donation systems and revolutionary financial planning to the homeless.
