Alex: Meet the Team Mondays

Alex: Meet the Team Mondays

Due to my father’s career, my family has moved around the world a lot. I have lived in many different cities and countries, which provided a very unique perspective to the world. Homelessness was an issue that I never recognized to be global until I lived in Japan and England. Japan has one of the lowest homeless rates in the world, but I still remember seeing individuals who had been displaced from their homes and living on the streets. Living in England, London to be more specific, I began to recognize that homelessness was a much bigger problem. I wanted to help those needing shelter, but I felt as if there was not much for me to do as a highschool student. However, there were small things that I learned I could do as a student. One of my most prominent memories from the UK was participating in the “Big Sleep Out” event where I slept outside in a box with hundreds of other people in support of ending homlessness. This was an important time in my life where I knew that I wanted to continue helping others find homes and get off the streets.

When I moved to Boston for college, I soon found that homelessness in Boston, like London, is everywhere and I am so glad to be a part of GiveCard which is working towards a solution to this growing problem.


Why GiveCard?


I joined GiveCard in 2018 after Lurien pitched the idea of the company to me. I was hooked ever since! GiveCard’s mission is simple. That is what I love about the company. We want to help the homeless community by providing financial freedom to those who need help in order to be best set up for success. Other than its mission, what I also love about GiveCard is the people. Everyone on the GiveCard team is excited and passionate about what we do which has a great impact on our work. GiveCard has the power to become a real solution to homelessness and I can’t wait to see how many lives GiveCard impacts in the future!
