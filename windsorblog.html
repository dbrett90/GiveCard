Windsor: Meet the Team Mondays

Windsor: Meet the Team Mondays

As I reflect back on my childhood, I have realized that my family has always been my source of inspiration for helping others. Although I chose a different career path, my parents and siblings work within the medical field has made a major impact on my approach to the world of business. Their dedication to relating and caring about each patient on a personal level has been something I strive to emulate in every aspect of my life. Although I grew up in Ohio, I have been fortunate enough to engage in service across the United States and around the world. These experiences furthered my passion for helping others, and helped me to better understand the needs of the marginalized. Therefore, by majoring in Finance and Accounting with a minor in Theology at Boston College, I believe that I can best utilize my God given gifts and talents to create systematic changes within society.


Why GiveCard?


If you look at advancements in technology over the last twenty to thirty years, American’s average quality of life has also increased. However, this is not the case for everyone, especially those marginalized by society. By creating this platform for the homeless community, GiveCard is solving systematic problems large institutions have failed to address. Overall, we are attempting to bridge the gap between satisfying individuals’ immediate needs and empowering them to create lasting lifestyle changes. By taking a hands on approach, we are aimed towards creating a program that understands each person’s individual needs and story. Therefore, by staying dynamic, we will be able to individual tailor our approach to each person’s needs.
