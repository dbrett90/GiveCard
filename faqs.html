We're Building the Next Generation Platform for Housing

What is Dryfter?

Dryfter is a network of properties designed specifically for remote workers and digital nomads. We buy large residential homes, outfit them with comfortable WFH amenities and rent them to groups that are working remotely.

How does Dryfter work?

Groups can rent Dryfter homes using our platform or through third party platforms. Our homes can also be used to host events such as corporate retreats, reunions, and even micro-weddings.

What can I expect in my home?

Dryfter homes come equipped with blazing fast wifi (1GBPS+), hi-res monitors, ergonomic desk chairs, and private offices. Our homes also come with amenities like firepits, grills, and hot-tubs. WFH has never been this fun.

What if I have a problem?

We have a support team available 24/7 to assist with any and all questions or concerns. Once a location is booked you will be assigned an individual specialist to support all your needs.

Are there COVID-19 Restrictions?

As of now there are no COVID restrictions in place, but we suggest guests abide by local guidelines. All properties come equipped with sanitizer and cleaning products which can be used at guests' discretion.

Will Dryfter buy my property?

Yes! If your property meets our needs we will make you a competitive offer. Reach out through our contact form with the property specs and a support specialist will reach out shortly.
